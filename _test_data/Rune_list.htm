This is the list of all the runes in Diablo II Expansion. Runes only impart their stats once they are socketed into an item. Also see runes and runewords.
  • To upgrade runes you are required to use 2 or 3 runes (plus a gem) of the level below the one you want to create in order to make 1 rune of the wanted rank. Recipes listed below.
  • All Runes have the same stats when in a Helm or Armor, and only two have different when in Shields.
  • Ilvl: A monster must be this level to drop the Rune.
  • Clvl: A character must be this level to equip an item socketed with this rune. Only the Hel (r15) Rune does not have a Clvl. Runewords almost always have a Clvl equal to that of the highest level rune in that Runeword.
  • In non-ladder, only upgrading with chipped and three stones works, higher than that you must find.
